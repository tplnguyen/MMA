A little bit about...

We, Lara Rondolo and Lan Nguyen, are Lifestyle Informatics students from the Vrije Universiteit, Amsterdam. We have been working on this project for about two years now, as a result of so-called procrastination. This project carried a few criteria, given by prof. Eliens, which was that the website needed to include a media viewer, an annotation editor (or something you can draw on) and a game (self invented, or in our case re-invented).

For our logo, we chose to draw a panda bear on a canvas using JavaScript. Why pandas? We both love pandas because they are adorable, so that was something we quickly agreed on.

Finally, we struggled a lot with what kind of theme we should go for. All about pandas? About travel, since we both love traveling? Food, we both work in the food industry? Why not combine it all? So that is what we did. We settled on the theme "our paradise", translating into what we think our paradise would look like.
